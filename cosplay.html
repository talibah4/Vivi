What is cosplay?

Cosplay is an acronym for "costume play" and is the practice of dressing up as a character from a movie, book, or video game, especially one from the Japanese genres of manga and anime. Cosplay is known world-wide and is mostly seen in the streets of Japan and during conventions such as Anime Expo, Comic Con, Dragon Con, Blitz Con, etc.
